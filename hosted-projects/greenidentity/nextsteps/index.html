Next Steps

We plan to start by establishing one TopPlay playground on Jameson Street in Parkdale. This location was chosen because Jameson Street is one of the poorest and most crime-ridden regions in Parkdale. It is also within walking distance to Queen Victoria Public School, so there will be many elementary school children living in those apartments. We hope to eventually expand and target other low socio-economic areas, such as Jane and Finch and Kipling Avenue on Rexdale, all over Canada. We are working on obtaining funding from corporate sponsors, such as TD Canada, Jays Care, President’s Choice Children’s Charity, and Nestle, as well as grants from the government.

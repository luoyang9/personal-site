TopPlay

TopPlay is a play area situated on apartment rooftops in low socio-economic areas that offers a safe and inviting environment for children ages 4-10. TopPlay provides users with playground equipments that have the ability to convert mechanical energy into electrical energy, a decorated area that encourages outdoor play ranging from chalk games to tag, and a garden for people who simply want to sit and relax. Any electrical energy harnessed from the playground equipments will be used to power LEDs illuminating TopPlay at night, security cameras, and the entrance sign-in device. Excess energy will be stored as the building’s backup power.

The design of each TopPlay facility is unique and adheres to the differing types of rooftops and their respective constraints (area, dimensions, pre-installed components, etc.). All of TopPlay's facilities will feature a playground and a garden situated on a green roof. A typical TopPlay playground includes a turf field, a gymnasium court, a garden, and a play area and is surrounded by a 4 m high dome-shaped fence. Our facilities are characterized by a unique security system known as SecurePlay and easily movable equipment that allows for optimized use of rooftop space.

Accessible

All children in the community, including those who do not live in the apartment complex, have the opportunity to access the TopPlay playground. However, children who do not live in the apartment complex need to be accompanied by a tenant of the building with TopPlay due to the username and password system. It is the tenant’s responsibility to ensure the privacy of their username and password.

Community Building

One of the major benefits of TopPlay is that it brings communities closer. Children in the neighbourhood are given the opportunity to play together and develop friendships in a safe and welcoming environment. Parents, guardians, and adults are also welcome to explore the garden area next to the playground and make new friends. The facility helps to foster positive relationships between the children, their parents, their neighbours, and subsequently, the entire community.

Adaptable

TopPlay uses portable playground and sports equipment. As a result, the playground offers a greater variety of sports for the children, depending on their own tastes. This equipment includes a merry-go-round, wind swings, a seesaw, a handheld seesaw, a modified fire pole, a stationary bikes station, and a RollerSeat slide. The playground can also be implemented on many differently shaped and sized apartments rooftops as none of the equipment is fixed on the ground.

Safe

TopPlay has an accessory security system (SecurePlay) that allows the parents of the children the ability to view the security footage in the playground when their children are logged into the facility.
